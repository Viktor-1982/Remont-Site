## 6. Renovate in Stages Instead of All at Once

Full-apartment renovation = chaos, noise, stress, budget spikes. Staged renovation lowers the risk of rework and improves control.

### Benefits:

- Fewer mistakes  

- Less stress  

- Budget spreads evenly  

- Easier quality control  

### Best order:

1. Bathroom and kitchen  

2. Bedroom  

3. Living room  

4. Hallway and balcony  

### Mini-block:

Up to **40% of renovation cost** comes from rework caused by incorrect sequencing. A proper plan eliminates most of that waste.
